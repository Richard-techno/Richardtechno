Chapter 1 basic grammar

Hi there! This is the first chapter of my conlang, Raito. Let's start by looking at the sentence Mok ry ka. It means I jump. Let's break it down. The word ry means I, and ka means jump. But the word mok is a bit different. It's a "tense word" that indicates the time frame of the action. Describing an action taking place in the present.
